About Yuganta

Yuganta is the vanguard of engineering excellence, pioneering innovation in the defense, mobility, and heavy machinery sectors. Born from a vision to shape the future, Yuganta is not just a brand — it's a movement redefining technological power and national resilience.

From advanced military-grade tanks and precision weapons to next-generation construction vehicles, trucks, and public transport systems, Yuganta is committed to delivering high-performance machines built with purpose. Every innovation we build is a reflection of strength, strategy, and sustainability.

Rooted in research, driven by purpose, and inspired by the possibilities of tomorrow — Yuganta is where power meets progress.
